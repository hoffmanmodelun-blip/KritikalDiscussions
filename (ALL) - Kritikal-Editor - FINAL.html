How to use this editor

1 · Find the page

Use the dropdown in the top bar to jump to any page (Home, Our Team, Debate Camp, and so on). In Preview mode you can also click the menu just like a normal visitor.

2 · Turn on Edit

Click Edit. Now anything you click becomes changeable. A bar at the bottom always tells you what's selected and what you can do with it.

3 · Change text

Click any heading or paragraph and just type. Click somewhere else when you're done.

4 · Swap a photo

Click a photo, then Replace photo in the bottom bar, and choose an image from your computer. Big images are shrunk automatically so the site stays fast.

5 · Fix a link or button

Click a button or link, then Edit link to change where it points (a Google Form, Instagram, an email, etc.).

6 · Add or remove a person / card

Click a team member, instructor, or card. The bottom bar offers Add another (makes an identical copy you can edit), Remove, and arrows to reorder. Copies always match the existing design.

7 · Made a mistake?

Press the Undo arrow in the top bar. And once published, the site keeps every old version on GitHub, so it can always be restored.

8 · Insert designed sections

Use Insert for ready-made text, photo, card, statistics, gallery, FAQ, timeline, and call-to-action layouts. They automatically match the website.

9 · Animate and style

Select something and open Design. Choose an entrance effect, timing, alignment, background, visibility, or page transition. Use Preview animation to test it.

10 · Create and manage pages

Use Pages to create a blank or complete project page, rename it, change its URL name, or show and hide it in the menus. Pages made by the editor can also be deleted safely.

11 · Publish

Click the green Publish changes button and follow the steps. Your changes go live in about a minute.
